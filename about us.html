About

Hi, I’m

Dr. Ranadip Halder !

Welcome to the page of Dr. Ranadip Halder, a renowned orthopaedic consultant with extensive experience in arthroscopy, arthroplasty, spine, and trauma surgery. With an MBBS with honours and MS in orthopaedics from R.G Kar Medical College & Hospital, a DNB in orthopaedics from MNAMS, New Delhi, and an MRCS England(I), Dr. Halder has gained specialized skills and knowledge in providing personalized care to patients from diverse backgrounds..

  • Our Mission

    The mission of Dr. Ranadip Halder is to provide the highest quality of orthopedic care to our patients. His committed to delivering personalized and comprehensive treatment plans that help our patients recover as quickly and safely as possible. He believes in building strong relationships with the patient, and his dedicated to providing an exceptional patient experience. His team is highly skilled and use the latest techniques and technologies to diagnose, treat, and manage a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions and injuries.
