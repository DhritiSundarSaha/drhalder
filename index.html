    My Work.

    Orthopaedic Consultant

    Orthopaedic Consultant with Extensive Experience: Dr. Ranadip Halder is a highly skilled and experienced orthopaedic consultant with expertise in arthroscopy, arthroplasty, spine, and trauma surgery. With years of experience working in top hospitals, Dr. Halder has successfully treated a wide range of orthopaedic conditions and injuries.

    Specialization in Arthroplasty

    Dr. Halder is a fellowship-trained arthroplasty specialist, having completed his fellowship in arthroplasty from Manipal Hospital. His expertise in joint replacement surgeries makes him a sought-after surgeon for patients with severe arthritis and other joint-related conditions.

    Trained in Top Institutions

    Dr. Halder has completed his MBBS with honours and MS in orthopaedics from R.G Kar Medical College & Hospital. He has also earned a DNB in orthopaedics from MNAMS, New Delhi, and an MRCS England(I). His training in top institutions has equipped him with the skills and knowledge necessary to provide high-quality orthopaedic care to his patients.
